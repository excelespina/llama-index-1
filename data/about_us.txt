About Us



Mission & Vision


MISSIONIt Takes A Village Academy’s (ITAVA) mission is to prepare students with the advanced skills, knowledge, and confidence to become positive and responsible global citizens by cultivating in them ITAVA’s four core values
COMMUNICATIONCHARACTERCRITICAL THINKINGCIVIC RESPONSIBILITY
VISIONEvery graduate will be prepared for college / career success as contributing members of a global society.
