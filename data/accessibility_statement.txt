Accessibility Statement





It Takes a Village Academy is committed to making our website accessible to visitors with disabilities and is continually working to increase the accessibility and usability of our site. We strive to conform to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0, Level AA. These guidelines outline how to make website content accessible to a wider range of people with disabilities. We are on a regular basis seeking opportunities to bring all areas of the site up to the same level of overall web accessibility.
If for any reason you cannot access any of the material on our website, please contact us and we will work to resolve the problem as soon as possible.
