9 - 12  Grade Curriculum



2021-2022 Focus Statement: Strengthen Core Instruction


By placing data at the core of improvement efforts, teachers will adopt a common set of instructional practices designed to support students' development of advanced literacy skills to promote college/career success
Daily learning experiences will include:Engaging TextsRich DiscussionDaily Writing High-Utility Vocabulary Words








Regents Classes


Our teaching staff takes pride in their ability to give students the ability to have college ready regents scores that all major CUNY and SUNY colleges and universities are looking for.




Extra Help


We offer many opportunities to students who may be struggling in their classes or would like to participate in an activity that gives them extra credit. Saturday school is also offered if students want to sharpen their skills in academics or athletics.




In-College Courses


Our school gives students the chance to gain a variety of college credits that will give them them the boost that they need to succeed. These courses will take place in colleges and the credits will be discounted or given for free.
